# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Argopia is a small project; let's keep it a place
people want to contribute to.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good faith. People come with different backgrounds and
  context.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect that the maintainer's time is limited — a slow response
  isn't a slight.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments — public or private.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block
accounts that violate this. Report concerns privately via
[GitHub private reporting](https://github.com/samson-ailabs/argopia/security/advisories/new)
or by opening an issue tagged `conduct` (the content can be minimal —
just enough to start a conversation).

This is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/);
the full text applies in principle where this short version is silent.
