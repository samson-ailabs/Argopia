# About Acme Voice AI

Acme Voice AI builds the speech-to-text and text-to-speech infrastructure
that powers voice agents at scale — over 1M audio hours processed daily
across our customer base. Our flagship product, Universal Streaming ASR,
powers real-time transcription for contact centers, voice agents, and
live captioning.

# About the Role

We're hiring a Senior ML Engineer to accelerate our research-to-production
pipeline. You'll bridge research innovation and production reliability,
working closely with our research team to ship state-of-the-art models
into our streaming infrastructure.

# Responsibilities

- Own end-to-end ML systems from research handoff through deployment,
  monitoring, and iteration.
- Build comprehensive observability solutions to monitor model
  performance and system health.
- Design cost-efficient inference pipelines that meet sub-300ms latency
  targets at scale.
- Scope solutions to ambiguous technical problems and help set the
  technical direction for the streaming team.
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to a high-throughput
  research-to-product cadence.

# Ideal Background

- 5+ years building production ML systems, ideally in audio / speech /
  language.
- Experience with PyTorch, distributed training, and inference
  optimization.
- Track record shipping research models into production at scale.
- Comfortable with observability tooling (Prometheus, Grafana,
  distributed tracing, structured logging).
- Audio domain experience is ideal but not required.

# Compensation

$195,000 - $225,000 base + equity + benefits. EMEA remote. We sponsor
visas for relocation to our Berlin / Dublin / Lisbon hubs if you
prefer hybrid.
