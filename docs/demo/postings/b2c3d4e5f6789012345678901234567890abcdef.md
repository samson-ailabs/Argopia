# About NovaSpeech Labs

NovaSpeech Labs builds enterprise voice agents for customer support. We
ship into Fortune 500 contact centers handling tens of millions of calls
per month. Our pipeline is streaming end-to-end — ASR → NLU → dialog →
TTS — with full observability across every stage.

# The Role

We're hiring a Senior Speech AI Engineer to lead the streaming ASR + TTS
pipeline. This is a build role: you'll own the production path from
model artifacts through deployed inference and back. Expect to spend
roughly 60% on engineering, 25% on production observability and
incident response, and 15% on research integration.

# What You'll Do

- Design and ship streaming ASR / TTS systems at sub-300ms end-to-end
  latency for 100+ concurrent voice agents.
- Build the observability, alerting, and rollback infrastructure that
  makes production safe.
- Partner with the research team on model handoff: evaluation harnesses,
  A/B infrastructure, regression testing.
- Set the technical direction for inference cost optimization as we
  scale to 10M+ daily calls.

# Background

- 5+ years production ML, ideally on real-time / streaming systems.
- Hands-on with PyTorch and at least one inference framework (Triton,
  TensorRT, ONNX Runtime).
- Comfortable owning systems end-to-end — you've been the on-call for
  something you built.
- Audio / speech domain experience strongly preferred but not strict.

# Compensation & Logistics

Globally remote with timezone flexibility (most teammates overlap 4+
hours with European working hours). Comp is competitive, equity
included; we share specific numbers in the first conversation.
